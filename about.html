About Rwanda Art Hub

Discover the story behind our creative sanctuary and our mission to showcase Rwanda's artistic heritage

Our Story

A Bridge Between Art & Culture

Founded in 2020, Rwanda Art Hub emerged from a vision to create a dedicated space where Rwanda's rich artistic traditions could be preserved, celebrated, and shared with the world. What began as a small gallery has grown into a vibrant cultural center that attracts visitors from across the globe.

Our journey started with a simple idea: to provide Rwandan artists with a platform to showcase their work and connect with art lovers. Today, we host over 200 local artists and have facilitated countless cultural exchanges that have enriched both our community and our visitors.

Through exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events, we aim to preserve Rwanda's artistic heritage while fostering innovation and creativity for future generations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower Rwandan artists by providing them with a global platform, fair-income opportunities, and the resources needed to preserve and innovate upon traditional art forms. We aim to make art accessible to all while promoting cultural understanding and appreciation.

Our Vision

To establish Rwanda as a globally recognized destination for art and cultural tourism, where visitors can deeply connect with the country's heritage through immersive artistic experiences that go beyond traditional tourism.

Our Core Values

Community First

We prioritize the needs and growth of our local artistic community, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable opportunities for all artists.

Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly art practices and use sustainable materials, respecting both our cultural heritage and natural environment.

Authenticity

We honor traditional techniques while encouraging contemporary interpretations, maintaining the authentic spirit of Rwandan art.

Global Connection

We bridge cultures by connecting Rwandan artists with international audiences, fostering cross-cultural understanding through art.
