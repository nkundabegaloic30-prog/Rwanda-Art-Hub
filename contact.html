Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our main line at +250 788 123 456.

Can I schedule a private tour outside of regular hours?

Yes, private tours can be arranged outside of regular hours for groups of 10 or more. Additional fees apply. Please contact our bookings department at least one week in advance.

Do you offer art shipping services internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping for art purchases. Shipping costs are calculated based on size, weight, and destination. We use professional art handlers and insured shipping services.

How can local artists submit their work for consideration?

Rwandan artists can submit their portfolio through our online submission form or by emailing art@rwandaarthub.rw. Please include high-quality images, an artist statement, and relevant background information.

Do you offer corporate event hosting or team building activities?

Yes, we offer custom corporate packages including private viewings, team building workshops, and event hosting. Contact our partnerships team for customized proposals.
