About Rwanda Art Hub

A Bridge Between Art & Culture

Rwanda Art Hub is a creative sanctuary that celebrates the vibrant artistic heritage of Rwanda. We showcase traditional and contemporary Rwandan art forms, from Imigongo patterns to modern paintings.

Our mission is to create a platform where local artists can showcase their work to the world, and where visitors can immerse themselves in Rwanda's rich cultural tapestry.

Through exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events, we aim to preserve Rwanda's artistic heritage while fostering innovation and creativity for future generations.

Explore Experiences

Unique Experiences

Imigongo Workshops

Learn the ancient art of Imigongo painting using cow dung and natural pigments. Create your own geometric masterpiece to take home.

Basket Weaving

Master the intricate techniques of Rwandan basket weaving (Agaseke) with guidance from local master weavers.

Artist Meet & Greet

Connect directly with Rwandan artists, learn about their inspiration, and see them at work in their studios.

Cultural Tours

Join guided tours that explore the history and symbolism behind Rwanda's most iconic art forms and cultural practices.

Our Mission

The Rwanda Art Hub acts as a bridge: it empowers Rwandan creators by giving them a global platform and fair-income opportunities, while giving travelers a meaningful reason to stay longer, dive deeper, and connect emotionally with the country beyond gorillas and genocide memorials.

We turn art into a new pillar of Rwandan tourism—one that is joyful, forward-looking, and unmistakably Rwandan. Our vision is to make Rwanda a globally recognized destination for art and cultural tourism.
