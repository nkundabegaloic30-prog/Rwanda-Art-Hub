Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

General gallery visits don't require advance booking. However, we recommend booking workshops, guided tours, and group visits in advance to ensure availability. You can book online through our website or by calling +250 788 123 456.

Is photography allowed?

Yes, photography for personal use is allowed in most areas of the gallery. Flash photography, tripods, and professional equipment require special permission. Some temporary exhibitions may have restrictions, which will be clearly indicated.

Are there dining options available?

Yes, we have a café on-site serving Rwandan coffee, tea, and light snacks. For full meals, there are several excellent restaurants within walking distance in Kacyiru.

Can I purchase artwork during my visit?

Absolutely! Most artworks in our gallery are available for purchase. Our staff can assist you with purchases, packaging, and shipping arrangements both locally and internationally.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounted rates for groups of 10 or more people. School groups, university students, and organized tours receive special rates. Contact our group bookings department for more information.

What languages are tours available in?

Our regular tours are available in English, French, and Kinyarwanda. Tours in other languages (German, Spanish, Chinese) can be arranged with advance notice. Audio guides are available in multiple languages.
